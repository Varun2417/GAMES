Angry Birds is a Finnish action, puzzle, and strategy based media franchise created by Rovio Entertainment, and owned by Sega.
The game series focuses on the titular flock of colorful angry birds who try to save their eggs from green-colored pigs.
Inspired by the game Crush the Castle,[1] the original game has been praised for its successful combination of fun gameplay, comical style, and low price.
